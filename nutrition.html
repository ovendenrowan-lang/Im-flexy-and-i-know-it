Pre‑Workout Nutrition

Fuel your body with carbs and light protein 1–2 hours before training.

  • Banana + peanut butter
  • Oats with berries
  • Greek yogurt + honey

Post‑Workout Nutrition

Focus on protein and carbs to repair muscle and restore energy.

  • Protein shake + banana
  • Chicken, rice, and vegetables
  • Egg omelette + wholegrain toast

Muscle‑Building Foods

  • Lean meats
  • Eggs
  • Oats
  • Rice & pasta
  • Greek yogurt
  • Salmon & tuna
